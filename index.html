Do we use handwriting these days?

In our modern world, especially nowadays when nearly everything is virtual, typing on the computer, phone or other devices has almost replaced handwriting. We keep our important documents on our computers, write e-mails instead of letters, do our shopping lists on applications and so on. We use pen and paper less and less each day. However, for some people, like me, handwriting still preserves its importance. I am talking about a different type of handwriting, the 'beautiful handwriting'

Why I wanted to learn Calligraphy

Calligraphy is composed of two Greek words for “beauty” (kallos) and “to write” (graphein). I was impressed by this art when my husband sent me a letter before our marriage. On the envelope, he wrote my name with a calligraphy pen. I wanted to learn this art and started to learn with the help of my husband. All I needed was a calligraphy pen, paper and ink.

Benefits of writing Calligraphy for me

I think it makes me feel great. First of all, it helps me relax when I am stressed. When I start writing, I become so concentrated that I forget about all the stressful events I went through. Moreover, I can write my friend’s names or their favourite quotations and give them to my friends as presents , which make them really happy. I am still not in advanced level, but I want to improve myself and become more talented on this area. You can read more about the benefits of calligraphy here!
